## Problem

I need a public place that shows what I am learning and building without turning into a stiff portfolio or a daily-content treadmill.

## What I built

This site is the start of that system: a Hugo site with sections for projects, WGU coursework, course retrospectives, and weekly work logs.

## What I learned

The useful part is not the theme. It is the structure around recurring work: projects, courses, and weekly notes.

## Current status

The first cleanup pass is in progress. Demo content is being replaced with real builder-site structure.

## Next steps

Add the first real course retrospective, fill in the official WGU course tracker, and connect project cards to real repositories as they are ready.
