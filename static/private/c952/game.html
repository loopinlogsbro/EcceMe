How to play

Each station drills one exam-relevant LEGv8 concept. Pick a station with the buttons above (or keys 1–4). Hit R to reroll the current round, Enter to submit, and 1–4 to pick a multiple-choice option. Your station scores and completion badges persist in your browser.

  • A · Decoder. Identify the LEGv8 instruction format (R / I / D / B / CB / IM) for a 32-bit encoding, then pick the disassembled instruction.
  • B · Registers. Click an X-register chip to select it, then click the ABI bucket it belongs to.
  • C · Flags. Toggle the NZCV bits the operation produces, then mark every B.cond branch that fires.
  • D · Addressing. Tag each LEGv8 snippet with its addressing mode, then submit.
